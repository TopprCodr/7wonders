Seven Wonders of the World

7 Wonders of the World was a campaign started in 2000 to choose Wonders of the World from a selection of 200 existing monuments.In 2007, more than 100 million people voted to declare the New Seven Wonders of the World. The following list of seven winners is presented without ranking, and aims to represent global heritage.
